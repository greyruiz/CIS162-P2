About This Site...

This site is a small portfolio of some of the music I've enjoyed over the years.
The amount of screenshots, notes, and saved playlists across my old and current devices is pretty ridiculous. Once in a while I'll really want to listen to a certain track, but I can hardly audiate it's structure or lyrics, let alone the title of the song. Recently I learned about the Personal Knowledge Management system. This small-scale 'second-brain' system is a great way to practice my code and keep track of my current favorites. Eventually I may expand upon it on a future personal project.
